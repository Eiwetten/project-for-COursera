About us


Tucked away on Clover Street since 2003, The Folded Page has been the beating heart of the Ashford reading community. We are proudly a member of the IBA and believe that every great book deserves to find the right reader. Our knowledgeable staff hand-pick every title on our shelves — no algorithms, no bestseller lists, just genuine love for good stories.


"The Folded Page is the only bookshop where I walk in for one book and leave with five — and somehow every single one is exactly what I needed." — Margaret T., loyal customer since 2008"

Books

The Midnight Library

Author:Matt Halg

Description

Between life and death there is a library. When Nora Seed finds herself there, she discovers a chance to undo her regrets and try out each life she might have lived. A profound, life-affirming novel about second chances and the things that make life worth living.

Price: $14.99 all prices include VAT


Pachinko

Author:Min Jin Lee

Description

A sweeping saga following four generations of a Korean family, Pachinko is an unforgettable story of identity, sacrifice, ambition, and loyalty. One of the most important novels of the 21st century

Price: $13.50 all prices include VAT

Tomorrow, and Tomorrow, and Tomorrow

Author:Barielle Zevin

Description

A dazzling story of friendship, creativity, and love between two video game designers over thirty years. This novel asks what it means to work alongside someone, to make something together, and to be truly seen.

Price: $15.99 all prices include VAT
